# LuxeLeadPro - Sales Commission Structure (UPDATED)

## Commission Model: MONTHLY RECURRING

**Why monthly recurring:** Reps earn every month their customer stays active. Incentivizes quality closes + customer retention.

---

## Commission Rates

### Intelligence Plan
- **Customer Price:** $249/month
- **Commission:** $75/month per customer (flat rate)
- **Per Customer:** $50/month (recurring)
- **Example earnings:**
  - 5 customers = $250/month commission
  - 10 customers = $500/month commission
  - 20 customers = $1,000/month commission

### Intelligence + Generation Plan
- **Customer Price:** $749/month
- **Commission:** $225/month per customer (flat rate)
- **Per Customer:** $150/month (recurring)
- **Example earnings:**
  - 5 customers = $750/month commission
  - 10 customers = $1,500/month commission
  - 15 customers = $2,250/month commission

### Enterprise Plan
- **Customer Price:** Custom ($2,000-$10,000+/month)
- **Commission:** 20% of monthly fee while customer is active
- **Per Customer:** Varies
- **Example:**
  - 1 Enterprise customer at $5,000/mo = $1,000/month commission

## Why This Is Better

### For You (The Business)
✅ Only pay commissions when revenue flows in
✅ Reps motivated to close QUALITY customers (not just quantity)
✅ Reps keep customers happy (recurring revenue incentive)
✅ Natural feedback loop: reps see what works/doesn't
✅ Sustainable long-term (no upfront payment risk)
✅ Customer retention improves (rep has skin in game)

### For Sales Reps
✅ Recurring income (build a book of business)
✅ Income grows as they close more (passive income over time)
✅ Rewarded for keeping customers happy
✅ Clear path to $1k-$4k+ monthly income
✅ No clawback risk (earn as long as customer stays)
✅ More sustainable than one-time commissions

---

## Payment Schedule

- **Calculated:** End of each month
- **Paid:** By 15th of following month via PayPal/ACH
- **Requirement:** Customer must be active and not churned
- **No clawback:** Rep keeps all commissions earned

---

## Customer Churn Impact

**If customer cancels:**
- Rep stops earning commission next month
- **This aligns rep incentive:** Keep customer happy or lose income

**Example:**
- Rep closes $749/month customer (gets $159.80/month)
- If customer leaves Month 3: Rep earned $159.80 × 3 = $479.40 total
- Rep now needs to close new customers to maintain income

**Result:** Reps naturally become customer success advocates.

## Why Monthly > One-Time

| Aspect | One-Time | Monthly |
|--------|----------|---------|
| **Upfront cost to you** | High ($2,397) | Low ($39.80-$159.80) |
| **Rep motivation** | Close and move on | Keep customer happy |
| **Your risk** | Pay upfront, customer churns | Only pay while active |
| **Customer quality** | Quantity focused | Quality focused |
| **Rep income ceiling** | Limited (one-time per customer) | Unlimited (scales with tenure) |
| **Feedback loop** | Weak (rep moves to next customer) | Strong (rep engaged in success) |
| **Sustainability** | Unsustainable long-term | Sustainable growth |

## Questions Reps Will Ask

**Q: If my customer cancels, do I lose all commission?**
A: No, you keep everything you earned. But your next month's income depends on them staying active. That's why keeping customers happy is important.

**Q: How many customers do I need to make good money?**
A: 10-15 quality customers = $1,000-$3,000/month recurring. That's a solid full-time income with flexibility.

**Q: Is there a cap on how much I can earn?**
A: No. Earn from unlimited customers. More customers = more recurring income.

**Q: Can I lose commission if a customer isn't "happy"?**
A: No clawback. If customer stays, you keep earning. Simple as that.
