## Mobile App Features (DETAILED)

### **Push Notifications (Our Advantage)**
- 🔔 "Hot lead alert! 92-score lead in $3.5M+ range just entered system"
- 🔔 "Daily briefing ready - check top 3 leads for today"
- 🔔 "Market update: Luxury listings down 5% week-over-week"
- 🔔 "Compliance reminder: Check DNC before contacting [name]"

### **Offline Access**
- View previously loaded leads offline
- Sync when connection restored
- Critical feature for agents showing homes in poor signal areas

### **Quick Actions**
- **Call:** Direct dial from lead card
- **Email:** Pre-filled templates (personalized by AI)
- **Text:** SMS from app (integrated with messaging)
- **Schedule:** Calendar integration (send agent availability)
- **Share:** Send lead to team member

### **AI-Powered Features**
- **Lead Score Explanation:** "92 score because: New buyer + $3.5M budget + luxury neighborhood preference + active in past 30 days"
- **Smart Follow-up Suggestions:** "You last contacted 5 days ago. Recommended: Follow-up call today"
- **Market Insights:** "This neighborhood: 15% fewer listings than last month. Urgency: HIGH"
- **Buyer Profile:** AI generates likely preferences, timeline, pain points

### **Real-time Syncing**
- Lead status updates sync across desktop + mobile instantly
- Team activity updates (if another agent contacted this lead)
- Commission updates visible in real-time

### **Performance Tracking**
- Daily KPIs: Calls made, emails sent, conversions
- Weekly trends: Which neighborhoods converting best
- Monthly ROI: Cost per acquisition by lead source
- Yearly goals: Progress toward targets
