LuxeLeadPro - Sales Commission Structure (UPDATED)

Commission Model: MONTHLY RECURRING

Why monthly recurring: Reps earn every month their customer stays active. Incentivizes quality closes + customer retention.

Commission Rates

Intelligence Plan

- Customer Price: $249/month - Commission: $75/month per customer (flat rate) - Per Customer: $50/month (recurring) - Example earnings: - 5 customers = $250/month commission - 10 customers = $500/month commission - 20 customers = $1,000/month commission

Intelligence + Generation Plan

- Customer Price: $749/month - Commission: $225/month per customer (flat rate) - Per Customer: $150/month (recurring) - Example earnings: - 5 customers = $750/month commission - 10 customers = $1,500/month commission - 15 customers = $2,250/month commission

Enterprise Plan

- Customer Price: Custom ($2,000-$10,000+/month) - Commission: 20% of monthly fee while customer is active - Per Customer: Varies - Example: - 1 Enterprise customer at $5,000/mo = $1,000/month commission

Realistic Earnings Scenarios (Annual)

Conservative Rep (3 customers/month average)

- Closes 5 Intelligence customers in Month 1-2 - Closes 3 Intelligence + Generation Plan customers in Month 3-6 - Total: ~$3,900 annual commission

Average Rep (5 customers/month average)

- Closes 10 Intelligence customers in Month 1-3 - Closes 5 Intelligence + Generation Plan customers in Month 4-6 - Monthly recurring by Month 6: $1,000/month = $12,000/year

Top Rep (8+ customers/month average)

- Closes 15 Intelligence customers in Month 1-2 - Closes 10 Intelligence + Generation Plan customers in Month 3-6 - Monthly recurring by Month 6: $2,000/month = $24,000/year

Payment Schedule

- Calculated: End of each month - Paid: By 15th of following month via PayPal/ACH - Requirement: Customer must be active and not churned - No clawback: Rep keeps all commissions earned

Customer Churn Impact

If customer cancels: - Rep stops earning commission next month - This aligns rep incentive: Keep customer happy or lose income

Example: - Rep closes $749/month customer (gets $159.80/month) - If customer leaves Month 3: Rep earned $159.80 Ã— 3 = $479.40 total - Rep now needs to close new customers to maintain income

Questions Reps Will Ask

Q: If my customer cancels, do I lose all commission? A: No, you keep everything you earned. But your next month's income depends on them staying active. That's why keeping customers happy is important.

Q: How many customers do I need to make good money? A: 10-15 quality customers = $1,000-$3,000/month recurring. That's a solid full-time income with flexibility.

Q: Is there a cap on how much I can earn? A: No. Earn from unlimited customers. More customers = more recurring income.

Q: Can I lose commission if a customer isn't "happy"? A: No clawback. If customer stays, you keep earning. Simple as that.

This model aligns everyone's incentives. Better for you, better for reps, better for customers.
