Frequently Asked Questions

What does "ZIP code exclusivity" mean? ▼
With the Exclusive Territory plan, you are the only LuxeLeadPro agent in your ZIP code. All AI-generated leads in that territory go exclusively to you. No other agent on our platform can receive leads from your ZIP code. This gives you a true competitive moat.
How are leads verified? ▼
Every lead goes through our multi-step verification process: AI behavioral scoring, contact validation, property interest confirmation, and budget qualification. We only deliver leads that meet our quality threshold for $1M+ markets.
Can I upgrade my plan later? ▼
Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time and the price difference will be prorated. Many agents start with Intelligence and upgrade to Exclusive Territory once they see the AI scoring results. Founding Agents keep their locked-in rate even when upgrading.
What is the Founding Agent program? ▼
The first 50 agents to sign up lock in today's pricing for life. No rate increases, ever. This is our way of rewarding early adopters who believe in the platform. Once all 50 spots are filled, the program closes permanently.
Is there a contract or commitment? ▼
No long-term contracts. All plans are month-to-month with a 30-day money-back guarantee. We believe in earning your business every month through results, not locking you into a contract.
