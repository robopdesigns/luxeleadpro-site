Frequently Asked Questions

When will I start receiving leads?
Ad campaigns launch within 48 hours. Most agents see their first leads within 3-5 days. Full volume (30-50/month) is reached by week 2-3 as campaigns optimize.
What if a lead scores below 60?
We only deliver leads scoring 60 or above. Lower-scored leads are either nurtured automatically by our AI until they qualify, or filtered out entirely.
Can I expand my territory later?
Yes. As a Founding Agent, you receive 25% off additional territories — $1,124/month per additional ZIP code (your founding rate of $1,499 with 25% discount). After the first 25 Founding Agent spots are filled, additional territories are $2,249/month (25% off the standard $2,999 rate).
What if I want to cancel?
No contracts. Cancel anytime. Your territory will be released and made available to other agents. However, your founding rate cannot be restored once cancelled.
How do I get support?
Territory owners get priority support. Email support@luxeleadpro.com or use the live chat on the dashboard. Response within 1 hour guaranteed.
Can another agent get leads in my territory?
No. Absolutely not. Your ZIP codes are exclusively yours for as long as you're an active subscriber. That's the entire point of the Territory plan.
What happens after the first 25 Founding Agents?
The Exclusive Territory price increases to $2,999/month for all new agents. Your founding rate of $1,499/month remains locked forever.
